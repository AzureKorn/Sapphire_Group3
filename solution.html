Solutions for Individuals

  • Implement a Firewall in your device to block hackers and threats as it can be can be maintained for 5-7 years.
  • Use a sturdy 2-Factor authentication as it's highly recommended and only takes a few minutes to implement.
  • Use a strong password for your account to reduce the risk of getting attacked.
  • Encrypt your data as it can only be accessible with the ones who have the decryption key.

  • Create a digital/printed learning material about the proper ways on how to protect your device from threats.
